# EasyTeamShuffle

EasyTeamShuffle is a Discord bot that quickly and fairly creates teams from members in a voice channel.

It is designed for gaming communities that need simple and fast team organization.

---

## Features

- Shuffle members in a voice channel
- Create teams by **team size** or **number of teams**
- Easy **Re-shuffle button**
- Clear and readable embed results
- Supports **Japanese and English commands**

---

## How It Works

1. Join a voice channel
2. Run a command
3. The bot automatically creates balanced teams

Example:

`/team_size 3`

or

`/team_count 2`
