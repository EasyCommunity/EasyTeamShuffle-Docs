# Features

EasyTeamShuffle provides simple and efficient tools for automatically creating teams in Discord voice channels.

---

## Automatic Team Shuffle

EasyTeamShuffle automatically creates teams using members currently connected to a voice channel.

This allows communities to quickly organize matches, scrims, or events without manually assigning players.

---

## Re-shuffle Button

After teams are created, a **Re-shuffle button** appears in the result message.

This allows users to reshuffle teams instantly without running the command again.

---

## Multi-language Commands

EasyTeamShuffle supports both **Japanese and English commands**, allowing communities from different regions to use the bot easily.

---

## Clear Team Display

Teams are displayed in a **clean and easy-to-read embed format**, making it simple to see how members were distributed.

The result message can also include an explanation of how the teams were divided.

---

## Designed for Community Events

EasyTeamShuffle is especially useful for:

* Game matches
* Scrims
* Community tournaments
* Voice channel events

It helps organizers create balanced teams quickly and efficiently.
