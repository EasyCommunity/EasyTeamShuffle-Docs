# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

EasyTeamShuffle ("the Bot") respects your privacy. This document explains what information may be processed when using the bot.

---

# Information Collected

EasyTeamShuffle does not store personal user data.

The bot may temporarily access the following information in order to function:

* Discord user IDs
* Discord usernames
* Voice channel member lists
* Server IDs

This information is only used to perform team shuffling and is **not stored permanently**.

---

# Data Storage

EasyTeamShuffle does **not store any personal data** in a database.

All data used during commands is processed **temporarily in memory** and discarded immediately after the command finishes.

---

# Third-Party Services

EasyTeamShuffle uses the **Discord API** to operate.

Your use of Discord is also subject to the Discord Privacy Policy.

This privacy policy may be updated in the future.
Any changes will be reflected on this page.
