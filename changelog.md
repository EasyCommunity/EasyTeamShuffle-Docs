# EasyTeamShuffle Changelog

All notable changes to EasyTeamShuffle are documented here.

---

## v1.5.0

### Added
- Re-shuffle button to quickly reshuffle teams
- Display the user who triggered the re-shuffle in the result embed
- 10-second cooldown for re-shuffle

---

## v1.4.0

### Added
- English command support

### Improvements
- Improved internal team distribution logic

### Fixed
- Fixed calculation issues introduced by English command support

---

## v1.3.0

### Added
- Explanation in the embed showing how teams were divided

---

## v1.2.0

### Improvements
- Improved embed layout and visual design

---

## v1.1.0

### Improvements
- Remaining players are now displayed as a team instead of extra members

---

## v1.0.0

### Added
- Japanese commands
- Automatic team creation and player distribution
